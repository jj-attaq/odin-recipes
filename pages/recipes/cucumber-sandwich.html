Keto Cucumber Sandwich


You will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 big cucumber
  • ham of some kind
  • a hard cheese
  • something spicy, sriracha will work, but I used urnebes

Let's make some sandwiches!

  1. start by slicing up the cucumber, make sure the slices aren't too thick or thin,
    about 3 - 4mm is fine
  2. slice your cheese too be the same thickness
  3. slice up your ham if need be as well, these can be thinner
  4. make sure you cut up the cheese and ham into around the size of a cucumber slice
  5. spread your spicy sauce/urnebes onto a cucumber slice
  6. slap the ham and cheese onto the urnebes
      it doesn't count unless you SLAP! them on!
  7. place another cucumber slice on top and serve!
  8. bonus points for toothpicks to hold it all together

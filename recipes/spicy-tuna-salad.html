Spicy Tuna Salad

You will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 can of tuna (make sure its stored in olive oil or brine! no soy or vegetable oil!)
  • 1 ripe organic avocado
  • 3 organic chili peppers
  • 50g of uncooked quinoa
  • turmeric powder
  • half an organic red onion
  • 1 pickle
  • 2 dried figs
  • 1 lime
  • mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

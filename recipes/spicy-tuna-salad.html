Spicy Tuna Salad

You will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 can of tuna (make sure its stored in olive oil or brine! no soy or vegetable oil!)
  • 1 ripe organic avocado
  • 3 organic chili peppers
  • 50g of uncooked quinoa
  • turmeric powder
  • half an organic red onion
  • 1 pickle
  • 2 dried figs
  • 1 lime
  • mustard
  • balsamic vinegar
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Now we move on to our recipe.

  1. put some olive oil in a small pot over a medium-high temperature on the stove
  2. while the oil warms up, rinse your quinoa
  3. put the rinsed quinoa into the pot and add in a spoonful of turmeric powder
  4. stir the quinoa, oil and turmeric together for a minute or two
  5. add in 100ml of water, stir everything up a little and let it sit

While we wait for the quinoa to cook, it's time to prepare the rest!

  1. chop up your onion, pickle, figs, and hot peppers into small pieces
  2. now we'll prepare the dressing, get a glass and spoon ready
  3. put a little bit of balsamic vinegar in the glass, about double the amount of olive oil, some salt and pepper,
    and a generous teaspoon of mustard, and squeaze half a lime worth of juice into the glass
  4. mix it all up
  5. peal, pit and then chop up your avocado into small chunks
  6. your quinoa should be done by now, once the water has more or less completely evaporated, you'll know it's ready
  7. put everything into a bowl
  8. drain out the oil from the tuna can (unless you think you didn't put enough for the dressing) and add it to your bowl as well
  9. mix everything up and serve!
